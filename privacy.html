Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 20, 2025

Last Updated: July 20, 2025

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

Wordle Unlimited does not require you to create an account or provide personal information to play the game. We do not collect names, email addresses, or other personal identifiers.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, we may automatically collect certain information, including:

  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • IP address (anonymized)
  • Pages visited and time spent on pages
  • Referring website
  • Device type (mobile, desktop, tablet)

1.3 Game Data

Game statistics and progress are stored locally in your browser using localStorage. This includes:

  • Games played
  • Win/loss statistics
  • Guess distribution
  • Current and maximum win streaks

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

2. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the game service
  • Improve user experience
  • Analyze website usage patterns
  • Display relevant advertisements
  • Ensure website security

3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

3.1 Essential Cookies

We use localStorage to save your game statistics locally on your device. This data is essential for the game functionality.

3.2 Analytics Cookies

We may use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with our website. Google Analytics uses cookies to collect information anonymously.

3.3 Advertising Cookies

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings.

4. Third-Party Services

4.1 Google AdSense

Our website displays advertisements served by Google AdSense. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to our site and/or other sites on the Internet.

For more information about Google's privacy practices, please review Google's Privacy Policy.

4.2 Netlify

Our website is hosted by Netlify. Netlify may collect certain technical information for hosting purposes. Please review Netlify's Privacy Policy.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties except:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect our rights and safety
  • To trusted third-party service providers (like Google AdSense) who assist in operating our website

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information, including:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • Secure hosting environment
  • Regular security updates
  • Minimal data collection practices

7. Your Rights and Choices

7.1 Local Data Control

You can clear your game statistics at any time by clearing your browser's localStorage or using your browser's privacy settings.

7.2 Cookie Control

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect website functionality.

7.3 Advertising Preferences

You can opt out of personalized advertising:

  • Visit Google Ads Settings
  • Use browser ad-blocking extensions
  • Enable "Do Not Track" in your browser

8. Children's Privacy

Our website is intended for general audiences and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information.

9. International Users

Our website is hosted in the United States. If you access our website from outside the US, your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the US.

10. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

11. Contact Information

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us through our website.
